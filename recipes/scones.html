Scones

Description

easy fruit scones are perfect for relaxed weekend baking. Serve with strawberry jam and cream

Ingredients

  • 450g self raising flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 75g butter
  • 75g sugar
  • 150g sultanas or any dried fruit of your choice
  • 250ml milk

Steps

  1. Warm the oven to 180 degrees celcius
  2. Sift the flour and baking powder into a bowl
  3. Rub in the butter with the tips of your fingers until it resembles breadcrumbs
  4. Stiry in the sugar and fruit
  5. Add the milk and form a dough
  6. Roll out to approx 2cm thickness
  7. Cut out round shapes using a cutter
  8. Place in the oven for 10 minutes
